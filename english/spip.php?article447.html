Simin Behbahani, Renowned Iranian Poet Accepts Simone de Beauvoir Prize for the One Million Signatures Campaign in Paris Ceremony

Tuesday 27 January 2009


Change for Equality: On January 21 2009, an award ceremony was held in Café Deux Magots, where the One Million Signatures Campaign was awarded the Simone de Beauvoir Prize for Women’s Freedom.

Simin Behbahani, renowned Iranian poet and one of the first supporters of the Campaign, went to Paris to take part in this auspicious event and to accept the Award given to the Campaign. Following are pictures of the event, taken by Maryam Afshari, Bahareh Majdzadeh, and Arash Naiemian, members of the Campaign in France.
