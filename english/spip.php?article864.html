ABC "This Week’s" Discussion on Women in the Middle East

Women of the Revolution

Tuesday 8 March 2011


Change for Equality:Christiane Amanpour, host of ABC’s This Week, discussed the role of women in the middle east and their struggles for democracy and women’s rights. “This Week’s”’ discussion on women in the Muslim world brought together Tina Brown, editor-in-chief of The Daily Beast and Newsweek; Egyptian writer and activist Dr. Nawal El Saadawi; Zainab Salbi of Women for Women International; and Sussan Tahmasebi, Iranian Women’s Rights Activist and founding member of the One Million Signatures Campaign.
