Message to One Million Signatures

Tuesday 20 February 2007

Your movement is admirable, your struggle (Campaign of One Million signatures) is immense and your systematic approached is well thought out. Bravo and Afarin to all of you. Your movement has the power to change the current foundation of injustice in Iran’s discriminatory laws and be recorded in history forever.


Doostan, Irani ha, hamvatanan,

Your movement is admirable, your struggle (Campaign of One Million signatures) is immense and your systematic approached is well thought out. Bravo and Afarin to all of you. Your movement has the power to change the current foundation of injustice in Iran’s discriminatory laws and be recorded in history forever.

I fully support your cause and will add your petition to www.helpnazanin.com I have created this site in honour of Nazanin Fatehi in Iran who is a victim of discrimination and injustice herself.

For rightfully defending herself against three rapists, she was sentenced to death by hanging and has suffered in prison for almost two years No one deserves to die for defending themselves, let alone a juvenile who was 17 years old at the time of the alleged crime.

I would like for all of you to support her by signing the petition that I started over 10 months ago which now has over 225,000 signatures worldwide. I am looking forward to the day when women stand shoulder to shoulder in equality with their male counterparts in Iran and show the rest of the world what Iranians really represent: A strong country made up of even stronger people.

Peace and love,

Nazanin Afshin-Jam

Human Rights Activist/ Singer, Songwriter
