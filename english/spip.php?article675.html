Continued Detention of Dorsa Sobhani

Saturday 3 April 2010


Change for Equality: Dorsa Sobhani activists working on the right to education and also with the One Million Signatures Campaign in Sari remains in Evin. Because of her belief in the Bahai’i faith Dorsa was banned from continuing her studies. Dorsa was arrested on March 7, 2010, and has only been allowed one visit with her family since her arrest. Officials have provided no explanation as to the reason for her arrest or the charges pending against her.
